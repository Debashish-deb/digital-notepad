## Recommendations

1. **Lab Knowledge** (combines Overview + Wet Lab)

   - Protocols & SOPs

   - Data
   - Writing
   - Meetings
   - Archive

3. **Orders & Administration**
   - Billing & Finance
   - Logistics & Shipping
   - Administrative Documents

4. **Social & Events**
   - Lab Photos
   - Events & Retreats
   - Visitor Records

### Category Size Recommendations

- Categories with <5 files should be merged into parent categories
- Categories with 5-30 files work as subcategories/filters
- Categories with 30-150 files work as visible side categories
- Categories with >150 files need search, sorting, and filters
- Categories with >500 files need pagination/virtualization
